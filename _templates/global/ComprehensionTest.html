Instructions

Experiment Structure

The experiment consists of four parts:

  • Part {{ part_no_delegation }}: 10 rounds of an interactive task where you make all the decisions yourself in each round.
  • Part {{ part_delegation }}: 10 rounds of an interactive task where you delegate all decisions to an artificial delegate. Before the task begins, you will provide instructions for the delegate to follow.
  • Part 3: 10 rounds of an interactive task where you decide before the part begins whether to delegate all decisions to the artificial delegate (as in Part {{ part_delegation }}) or make all decisions yourself (as in Part {{ part_no_delegation }}).
  • Part 4: A guessing game (non-interactive), where you can earn up to $1 extra based on your answers.

Between each round of the interactive tasks (Parts {{ part_no_delegation }}, {{ part_delegation }}, and 3), you will be randomly rematched with a different participant. This means you will interact with many different people, not the same person throughout.

How the Interactive Tasks Work (Parts 1–3)

In each round, you and another participant will make decisions simultaneously. Your earnings depend on both your choice and the other participant's choice.

Table of Earnings (in Ecoins):

Your Choice \ Other's Choice Option A Option B
Option A 70 0
Option B 100 30

The numbers in the table represent your earnings in Ecoins for each combination of choices made by you (left column) and your opponent (top row). Exchange rate: 10 Ecoins = 1 cent.

Important:

Every participant you will interact with received the same set of Instructions as you.

There is no deception in this experiment.

Your answers will remain anonymous to your co-players throughout this experiment.

After each part, you will see your cumulative earnings from that part in Ecoins.

One part (10 rounds) will be randomly selected at the end to determine your bonus payment for the interactive tasks.

In each round, you can earn up to 100 Ecoins (10 cents). Since one part (10 rounds) will be selected for payment, you can earn up to $1 from the interactive tasks.
