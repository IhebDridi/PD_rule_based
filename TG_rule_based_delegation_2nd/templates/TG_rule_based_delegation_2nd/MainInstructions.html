Between each round of the interactive tasks (Parts 1–3), you will be randomly rematched with a different participant. This means you will interact with many different people, not the same person throughout.

How the Interactive Tasks Work (Parts 1–3)

In each round, you and another participant will make decisions that affect each other's bonus payments, according to the scheme below.

Specifically, in this task, there are two roles: the 1st mover and the 2nd mover. You will only know the role that was attributed to you at the end of each Part 1, 2 and 3, when you will also get to know the other participants' decisions and your resulting bonus payment for each round in Ecoins.

The earnings in Ecoins will be exchanged for dollars at a rate of 10 Ecoins = 1 cent.

Scheme of earnings:

The way to interpret this scheme is the following:

  • The 1st mover can choose either A or B:

    • If the 1st mover chooses B, both players get 30 Ecoins.

    • If the 1st mover chooses A, then the 2nd mover can choose either A or B:

      • If the 2nd mover chooses A, both players get 70 Ecoins.

      • If the 2nd mover chooses B, the 1st mover gets 0 Ecoins and the 2nd mover gets 100 Ecoins.

All participants will be asked to make decisions in both cases for each round: as the 1st mover and as the 2nd mover. However, as stated above, their true role in the game will be assigned randomly, and they will only learn it at the end of Parts 1, 2, and 3, when they also learn the decisions made by the other participants in those rounds and the resulting bonus payments.

For your convenience, the scheme of earnings will be shown to you at the end of the page, every time you have to make decisions in the game.

Important:

  • Your answers will remain anonymous to your co-players throughout this experiment.
  • After each Part 1, 2, and 3, you will see your cumulative earnings from that part in Ecoins.
  • One part (10 rounds) will be randomly selected at the end to determine your bonus payment for the interactive tasks.
  • In each round, you can earn up to 100 Ecoins (10 cents). Since one part (10 rounds) will be selected for payment, you can earn up to $1 from the interactive tasks.

Guessing Game (Part 4)

After the interactive tasks, you will answer questions about others' choices in Part 3. Your earnings in this part depend on the accuracy of your answers and can add up to $1 extra to your total bonus.

Summary of Earnings

  • Base payment: $4 (fixed).
  • Bonus from interactive tasks: Up to $1 (based on the randomly selected part).
  • Bonus from Guessing Game: Up to $1 (based on your answers).
  • Total possible earnings: $4 + up to $2 in bonuses.

What to Do Next

  1. You will first complete a comprehension test to ensure you understand the instructions.
  2. After passing, you will begin Part 1 of the interactive tasks.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions for each part.

Please take your time to understand the instructions provided. If anything is unclear, you may review these instructions at any time during the comprehension test.
