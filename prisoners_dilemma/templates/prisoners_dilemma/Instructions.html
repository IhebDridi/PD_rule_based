This experiment has four parts:

  • Parts 1 and 2: You will play 10 rounds each. In one part you make every decision yourself; in the other you delegate decisions to an agent. You will see which is which when you start.
  • Part 3: You choose whether to delegate or play yourself, then play 10 rounds.
  • Part 4: A short guessing game about other participants’ choices. You can earn a small bonus.

In each round of Parts 1–3 you are matched with another participant. Your earnings depend on your and their choices. 10 Ecoins = 1 cent.
