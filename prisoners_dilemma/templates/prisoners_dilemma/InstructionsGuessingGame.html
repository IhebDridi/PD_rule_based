Welcome to Part 4: Guessing game

In this part of the experiment, the task is not interactive. You will be playing alone. Your task is to tell us, for each round played in Part 3, if you think your co-player in that round delegated or not to an AI.

On the next page, we will present you with a table with the decisions your different co-players made in each round. You will then have to guess whether they were delegating or not in that round. On top of the column, you will see the question: “Did they delegate?” and you can answer “Yes” or “No” for each round of play.

Remember that you encountered different co-players in different rounds, and they all, just like you, were presented with the choice to delegate or not in Part 3.

For each correct answer, you will be rewarded with a 0.1$ bonus, so that you can earn up to 1$ extra for this part of the experiment.
