Welcome to Part {{ part_no_delegation }}: No Delegation

In this part of the experiment, you will make all decisions yourself for each of the 10 rounds. Unlike other parts, there is no delegation.

In each round, you will decide whether to choose A or B. For your convenience, the table of earnings will appear whenever you need to make a decision.

Once you are satisfied with your decision for that round, click “Confirm”, and you will move to the next round.

You might need to wait for your different co-players to finalize their answers before you are able to see the Results table.

At the end of this Part, you will see the decisions of your other co-players and your corresponding earnings in Ecoins for each round played.
