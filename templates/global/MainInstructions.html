Instructions

Welcome to the Experiment!

Thank you for participating. Please read these instructions carefully. Your earnings depend on your decisions and those of others. Every player received the same instructions, so that you all have the same information.

Experiment Structure

The experiment consists of four parts:

  • Part {{ part_no_delegation }}: 10 rounds of an interactive task where you make all the decisions yourself in each round.
  • Part {{ part_delegation }}: 10 rounds of an interactive task where you delegate all decisions to an artificial delegate. Before the task begins, you will provide instructions for the delegate to follow.
  • Part 3: 10 rounds of an interactive task where you decide before the part begins whether to delegate all decisions to the artificial delegate (as in Part {{ part_delegation }}) or make all decisions yourself (as in Part {{ part_no_delegation }}).
  • Part 4: A guessing game (non-interactive), where you can earn up to $1 extra based on your answers.

Between each round of the interactive tasks (Parts 1–3), you will be randomly rematched with a different participant. This means you will interact with many different people, not the same person throughout.

How the Interactive Tasks Work (Parts 1–3)

In each round, you and another participant will make decisions simultaneously.

Your earnings depend on both your choice and the other participant's choice, but you do not know your opponents choice at the time of making yours.

The numbers in the table below indicate your earnings in Experimental Coins (Ecoins), which depend on the options you and your co-player choose. Specifically, each number inside the table represents your earnings, as a result of you choosing the Option in the left column of that table entry, and your opponent choosing the Option on the top row.

These earnings appear in Ecoins, but they will be exchanged for dollars at a rate of 10 Ecoins = 1 cent.

Important:

  • Your answers will remain anonymous to your co-players throughout this experiment.
  • After each interactive task part (1-3), you will see your cumulative earnings from that part in Ecoins.
  • Only one of these parts (10 rounds) will be randomly selected at the end to determine your bonus payment for the interactive tasks.
  • In each round, you can earn up to 100 Ecoins (10 cents). Since one part (10 rounds) will be selected for payment, you can earn up to $1 from the interactive tasks.

Guessing Game (Part 4)

After the interactive tasks, you will answer questions about others' choices in Part 3. Your earnings in this part depend on the accuracy of your answers and can add up to $1 extra to your total bonus.

Summary of Earnings

  • Base payment: $3 (fixed).
  • Bonus from interactive tasks: Up to $1 (based on the randomly selected part).
  • Bonus from Guessing Game: Up to $1 (based on your answers).
  • Total possible earnings: $3 + up to $2 in bonuses.

What to Do Next

  1. You will first complete a comprehension test to ensure you understand the instructions.
  2. After passing, you will begin the first interactive task (Part {{ part_no_delegation }} or Part {{ part_delegation }}, depending on your condition).
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions for each part.

Please take your time to understand the instructions provided. If anything is unclear, you may review these instructions at any time during the comprehension test.
