Welcome to Part {{ part_delegation }}: Delegation

In this part of the experiment, you will delegate your decisions to an AI agent. This means that instead of making individual decisions in each round yourself, you will prompt an AI agent through a chatbox that will make the decisions on your behalf for all 10 rounds in this part.

You will first delegate your decisions in case you are the 1st mover; and then delegate your decisions in case you are the 2nd mover and the other player chose A as the 1st mover. Remember, even though you will be delegating decisions for both roles, your true role in each round will only be determined (randomly for each part) at the end of the 10 rounds.

How This Works

  1. Prompt your AI agent: You will use a chat box to write a prompt describing how you want the AI agent to make decisions throughout the 10 rounds of the game. This prompt can include any guidelines or preferences you want the agent to follow during the task.
  2. Agent Execution: Based on your prompt, the AI agent will determine which choice to make in each of the 10 rounds of play for that specific role. You can check how your prompt translates into behavior by checking the round-by-round decisions on the table that will appear on the right side of the chat window with your AI agent.
  3. Reshuffled co-players: Once again, you will be matched with a different co-player in each round, meaning that you might have to wait for them to also finalize their answers.
  4. Once you are satisfied with your decision: click "Confirm" to finalize your agent's settings. The AI agent will use your instructions to make decisions for you across all 10 rounds, according to the table generated from your prompt, for the role you are assigned to in each round. Once submitted, you will not be able to adjust the AI agent's decisions once the 10 rounds begin.
